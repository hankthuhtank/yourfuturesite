FROM OUR FAMILY TO YOURS

Cooper & Reilly Pickle

We’re a couple who decided to bring a little piece of the coast to our hometown. Every dish that leaves our kitchen is made with the same care we’d give our own family.

We believe great seafood shouldn’t require a plane ticket. It should feel like a small vacation right here in Paris, Texas — familiar enough to feel like home, special enough to make you slow down and savor it.

Fresh, high-quality seafood takes time. We prepare everything with attention to detail, and we’re always happy to adjust spice, swap sides, or create something that reminds you of a favorite trip. Just ask.

“Y’all are everything. Every visit, every order, every kind word helps keep this dream alive.”
— Reilly & Cooper Pickle
