the mom & pop part

A sweet stop on 6th Street

Pull up a chair on the porch

Brandi's is the Drewery family's little shop in downtown Greybull — equal parts candy counter, ice cream parlor, sandwich shop, and gift store, all under one red brick roof.

Travelers heading to and from Yellowstone have a way of stopping in for a quick treat and staying for lunch. There's a welcome bear carved out front, a picnic table under the umbrella, and folks behind the counter who are glad you came.
