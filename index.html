Our Story

Baked with love in Boulder.

The Sweet Spot is Boulder, Montana's beloved independent café where genuine warmth meets exceptional coffee and freshly baked everything. Owner Kayla brings heart, talent, and a serious passion for pastry to every single item.

From legendary cinnamon rolls the size of your head to silky espresso drinks, huckleberry lattes, and custom occasion cakes that blow people away — this is more than a coffee shop. It's a community gathering place.
