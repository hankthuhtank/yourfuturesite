The Artist Behind The Lens

Hi, I’m Elizabeth.

Born and raised in Navasota, TX, I’ve had a passion for photography since my junior year of high school. While I took a break to build my family and focus on teaching, I’m thrilled to be back doing what I love.

There’s nothing more rewarding than seeing my clients’ faces light up and capturing the warmth, love, and connection in every photograph. For me, this isn’t just a business—it’s a true source of joy.
