Hi, I’m Elizabeth.

Born and raised in Navasota, TX, I’ve had a passion for photography since my junior year of high school. After stepping away to build my family and focus on teaching, I’m thrilled to be back doing what I love most.

Photography has always been my creative outlet. There’s nothing more rewarding than seeing my clients’ faces light up and capturing the warmth, love, and connection in every photograph.

For me, this isn’t just a business — it’s a true source of joy.

I’m honored to help you create memories that last a lifetime.
