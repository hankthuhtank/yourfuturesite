Pull up a chair

A hole-in-the-wall
worth the drive.

The Pickled Porch is a women-owned deli on South Main, the kind of place where the walls are covered in odds and ends and the screen door never stops swinging.

The tri-tip is dry-seasoned, barrel-smoked, and sliced thin. The sandwiches come with names like the Classic Ma Rita and the Snarky Bird, and most of them are big enough for two. Pair one with a salad, a root beer, or a margarita and find a shady spot on the porch.

“Bring an appetite or a doggie bag — the sandwiches are huge.”

We’re right in downtown Angels Camp, across from the ice cream shop, in the heart of Calaveras County’s Gold Country. Come watch small-town life go quietly by.
